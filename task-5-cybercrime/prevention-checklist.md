# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown payment requests  
- Always verify links before clicking  
- Avoid downloading apps from unknown sources  
- Use strong passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication  
- Regularly check your bank transactions  
- Do not trust unknown calls claiming rewards or offers  

## 💰 UPI Safety Tips
- Remember: You never need to enter PIN to receive money  
- Always double-check receiver details before sending money
