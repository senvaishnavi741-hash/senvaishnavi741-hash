# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## ✅ Do's
- Keep your profile professional  
- Use strong privacy settings  
- Share useful and positive content  
- Verify information before posting  
- Respect others online  

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive data  
- Avoid posting inappropriate content  
- Do not spread fake news  
- Avoid using abusive language  
- Do not engage in online conflicts.
