# Task 1 Brief Description

This infographic was created using Canva. It is titled **Digital Literacy Awareness** and explains the meaning and importance of digital literacy for students. The design includes major topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools, email etiquette, and building a professional online presence.

The infographic highlights how students can use digital technologies effectively for communication, learning, and productivity. It also spreads awareness about online safety through points like protecting privacy, using strong passwords, and avoiding scams or fake news. In addition, it encourages students to maintain a professional online image and follow proper email etiquette.

This visual resource was designed to be simple, informative, and easy to understand for college students.
